Rating: 8.5

Late Night with the Devil is an interesting cross between found footage and an occultic horror. The idea is that we're watching unreleased footage from a late night show from the 70's. However, some of the footage just doesn't make sense at all for that premise and is shot like we're just watching a regular movie. It's not very distracting though, the story is way too interesting to be bothered by that.

You might like this movie if you liked The Exorcist or Color Out of Space.
