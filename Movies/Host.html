Rating: 7

Disclaimer! This is a found footage movie! In fact, we found this movie while specifically searching for found footage movies. I think the story goes that these people were planning on making a found footage movie already, but then covid hit. So rather than put it off, they decided to make a movie remotely. The biggest issue with this is that being completey on a Zoom call, I don't always believe their reason for filming. You don't normally pick up your laptop and carry it in front of you when you're investigating somthing in your house. I chose to suspend my disbelief because they actually did a great job with the story and the scares, it's a great watch!

You might like this movie if you liked Paranormal Activity or Truth or Dare.
