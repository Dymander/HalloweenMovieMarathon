Rating: 8.5

This movie might not be for everyone, but it resonates with me, I just love it. It's very much a 2000's movie, which wins points from me but probably loses points for most. I think it has a great setting that gives a somewhat summer-vacation or dream-like vibe to everything. I think that ties into the ending, but I was gripped by the story all the way through.

You might like this movie if you liked The Wretched or What Lies Beneath.
