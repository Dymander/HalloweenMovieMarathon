Rating: 6

Obviously inspired by The Omen, but I liked the direction this movie took. In a way, I almost see it as the anti-Omen, like 3/4 of the way through, you'll see why. They chose to be less supernatural and more "belief-driven people going way too far," which I actually liked. This movie is not afraid to make the viewer uncomfortable, but they do it intentially.

You might like this movie if you liked The Omen, Midsommar, or Rosemary's Baby.
