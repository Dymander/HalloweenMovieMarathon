Rating: 6.5

I was pleasantly surprised with Winchester (be warned, this is a jumpscare movie.) If you're familiar with the Winchester mansion, it's as if they took that local legend, took away the mystery and cranked up the scares. It's a little bit action by the end as well, but it works. If you're into the jumpscare genre, give this one a shot!

You might like this movie if you liked Insidious, The Awakening, or Smile.
