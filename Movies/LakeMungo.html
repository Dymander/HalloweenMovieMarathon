Rating: 5

Disclaimer! This is a found footage movie! Actually it's more like a mocumentary, which I first thought was a bit of an odd choice for a horror movie. However, as I watched, I found that it held my interest the whole way through. That's not to say it was the most gripping story ever, but it really was worth it for the ending. I don't see myself rewatching it all the time, but I think everyone should see it at least once.

You might like this movie if you liked The Fourth Kind, The Blair Witch Project, or the Hell House LLC series.
