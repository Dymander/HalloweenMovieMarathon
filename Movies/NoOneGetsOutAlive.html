Rating: 6

This movie does a great job of mixing horror into a desperate situation. I really loved the setting of this movie and how it progressed. Some of the scary elements predictably drop off towards the end, but it makes up for it with a solid story and a great ending. Not a movie that has a lot of rewatch value, but definitely worthy of seeing at least once.

You might like this movie if you liked The Ritual or Last Night in Soho.
