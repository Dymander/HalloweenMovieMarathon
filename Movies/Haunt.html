Rating: 8

Haunt is a great execution of a modern slasher. It's the classic "group of teens/20-somethings vs a serial killer" or in this case, killers. However, this movie drops the supernatural and focuses on what you would acutally have to do in this situation. I don't think that kind of movie always works for horror, but this movie does a great job. I think the haunted house attraction was an awesome setting for this kind of story. I'm kind of surprised I haven't heard of another movie doing this.

You might like this movie if you liked Hell House LLC, Thanksgiving, or Escape Room.
