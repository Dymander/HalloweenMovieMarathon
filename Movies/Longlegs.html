Rating: 7

A wonderful crime thriller with supernatural and occultic elements mixed in. This movie likes to play with a lot of fun paranormal conepts. It's one of those "what if law enforcement had to deal with a crime being committed with supernatual means." It's a little weird that there are other movies and shows that do a similar concept, but they're almost always great. The twist they take on this one is that both the good and bad guys have some supernatual powers. I have some gripes with the ending, which lowered my rating of it, but I really did love this one.

You might like this movie if you liked The Empty Man, The Outsider TV series, or Stir of Echoes.
