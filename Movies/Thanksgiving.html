Rating: 9

This might be the most well-executed slashers I've ever seen. It's silly, it's thrilling, it has everything you want in a slasher and more! My expectations were incredibly low for this movie, but I had a blast watching it. It's fun enough that it has a high rewatch value and it fits the season perfectly (besides being about a November holiday.) I don't mean to overhype it, but seriously give it a watch!

You might like this movie if you like classic slashers (Friday the 13th, Nightmare on Elm Street, etc.,) Scream, or Truth or Dare.
